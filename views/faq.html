Frequently Asked Questions

What services does the HealthCare Portal provide?

We provide appointment booking, doctor profiles, emergency contact information, and access to health records.

How do I book an appointment?

You can book an appointment by navigating to the "Book Appointment" section on the homepage and selecting your preferred doctor and time slot.

Can I update my profile information?

Yes, you can update your profile by visiting the "My Profile" section and selecting the "Update Profile" option.

What should I do in case of an emergency?

In case of an emergency, visit the "Emergency Contacts" section for details on ambulances, hospitals, and helplines.

How can I reset my password?

You can reset your password by clicking on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page and following the instructions.

Is my data secure?

Yes, we prioritize your data security by following industry-standard encryption and protection measures.
